Pixelated Realms is a video game podcast I co-host with my brother Tyler and our friend Dustin. What started as an excuse to talk about games with people I actually like has grown into a consistent weekly show with a real audience and a community we're proud of.

## What We Cover

Every episode is a mix of what we're playing, what's happening in the industry, and whatever we can't stop talking about that week. We don't take ourselves too seriously — the show is conversational by design, the kind of thing you put on during a commute or a long gaming session.

- **Now Playing** — we each share what we've been playing and what we think of it, no review scores required
- **News & Headlines** — the week's biggest stories, announcements, and discourse
- **Deep Dives** — occasional longer-form episodes dedicated to a single game, franchise, or topic
- **Guest Interviews** — conversations with developers, content creators, and other interesting voices from across the industry

## My Role

I produce and co-host the show — that means handling the technical side (recording setup, editing, publishing, the website) as well as being on the mic every week. It's been a good forcing function for getting better at audio production, web presence, and just showing up consistently.

## The Show

New episodes drop every Tuesday wherever you listen to podcasts and on YouTube. We've been running since June 2023 and have no plans to stop.
